In order to use Jmoribus as a dependency you have to add the following to your `pom.xml`.

```xml
<dependency>
	<groupId>nl.eernie.jmoribus</groupId>
	<artifactId>jmoribus-core</artifactId>
	<version>0.3</version>
</dependency>
```
